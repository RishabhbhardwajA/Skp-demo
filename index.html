About Us

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Nurturing minds, building futures since 1980

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

SKP Government Degree College, established in 1980, stands as a beacon of higher education in the Rayalaseema region of Andhra Pradesh. Named after the legendary ruler Sri Krishnadevaraya, our institution has been committed to providing quality education to students from all walks of life.

With a strong focus on academic excellence, holistic development, and social responsibility, we have successfully nurtured thousands of graduates who are now making significant contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of higher learning that produces competent, socially responsible, and globally competitive graduates who contribute to the sustainable development of the nation.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education accessible to all sections of society
  • To create a conducive academic ambience for holistic development
  • To develop global competence among students through innovative teaching
  • To inculcate values of integrity, accountability, and social responsibility
